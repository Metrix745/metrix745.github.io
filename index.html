About Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Maryland, Baltimore County with a B.S. in Computer Engineering and a B.A. in Mathematics. Currently, I am looking for research opportunities in the field of robotics, machine learning, and brain-computer interfaces.

Passionate about harnessing the power of computer science to address complex challenges, I am driven by curiosity to innovate and make a positive impact. With professional experience in diverse domains, like robotics, semiconductors, and pollution to personal projects involving 3D printing and urban planning, I thrive on finding transformative solutions at the intersection of technology and real-world problems.
